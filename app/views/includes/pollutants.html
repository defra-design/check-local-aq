Gases

Produced by the burning of fossil fuels from sources such as cars, power plants and factories.

  • Ozone
  • Nitrogen dioxide

Particulate matter (PM)

Particulate matter are tiny pieces of solids or liquids in the air which come from sources such as car tyres, brakes and exhaust dust and wood burning and pollen.

  • PM 2.5
  • PM 10
