Air pollution breaches

The Air Quality Standards Regulations (2010) sets out the legal safe limits for specific air pollutants.

When a pollutant goes over (breaches) its legal safe limit in one area, it may be a serious health risk to people nearby. Find out what to do if you’re in an area with an active air pollution breach.
