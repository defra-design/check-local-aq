Nitrogen dioxide is a brown gas that can affect our health and how well our lungs work. People with asthma are particularly affected.

Sources of nitrogen dioxide

Nitrogen dioxide is released into the atmosphere when fuels are burned. It's mainly produced during:

  • burning of petrol or diesel in a car engine
  • burning natural gas in a central-heating boiler or power station
  • welding
  • the use of explosives
  • commercial manufacturing
  • food manufacturing
