Sources of nitrogen dioxide

Nitrogen dioxide is a colourless gas. It's mainly produced during:

  • burning of petrol or diesel in a car engine
  • commercial manufacturing
  • food manufacturing

Health effects 

  • asthma
  • respiratory infections
  • symptoms of people with lung or heart conditions could be made worse

Children who are exposed to nitrogen dioxide over a long period of time have an increased risk of respiratory infection and may have poorer lung function in later life.
