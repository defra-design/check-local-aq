How this council is reducing air pollution

This council’s priorities for reducing exposure to air pollution are: 

  • Clean Air Zone Implementation and Operation
  • Work with schools and nurseries in the Jewellery Quarter - Implement the Transport for West Midlands 'Travel for Life' Programme
  • School Streets: establishing permanent School Streets where traffic and parking are restricted during peak times to reduce engine idling and encourage walking and cycling
  • EV Infrastructure: installing 130 new fast and rapid electric vehicle charging points to support the transition from petrol and diesel

Find the website for this council for other ways they are reducing air pollution.

Smoke control areas

Smoke control areas are zones where it’s illegal to emit smoke from a chimney or buy or sell unauthorised fuel for use within them.
