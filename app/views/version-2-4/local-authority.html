Air Quality Management Areas (AQMAs)

An AQMA is declared when air quality objectives for specific pollutants are not being met or are unlikely to be met. Local authorities must create action plans to improve air quality in these areas.

Smoke control areas

Smoke control areas are zones where it’s illegal to emit smoke from a chimney or buy or sell unauthorised fuel for use within them.
